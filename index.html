Introspective Music


A rapper, singer and songwriter from Leeds who pulls inspiration from a variety of genres including elements of emo-rap and grime as well as referencing ‘70s soul and ‘00s R&B.

ATO is an artist who strives to be vulnerable and honest in his music, whether it be about his childhood or his intentions in romantic relationships ATO raps from a place of cathartic self-recognition. Only child ATO comes from a first generation of immigrants – his mum being from Denmark and his dad from Ghana.

The introspection of ATO’s music with his reflections on life lessons and memories is what makes fans stay. In an interview with DJ mag ATO stated that:

“I’ve just let go and tried to be honest. I can't on the one hand be trying to tell a story about untold stories while not being genuine about who I am on the other. It's important to put myself out there, to be able to give an honest reflection of this experience that I've had and that a lot of other young people have had. I don't really think about the anxiety or the consequences of it in the moment; it feels almost like it just has to be.”
